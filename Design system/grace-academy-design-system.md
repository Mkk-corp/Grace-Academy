## Logo

### Full Logo

![Grace Academy Full Logo]- in the same folder

**Components:**
- **Emblem** — Heraldic shield featuring an open book and a golden silhouette of a head with a puzzle piece, symbolising knowledge and critical thinking
- **Laurel wreath & banner** — Classic academic motif conveying prestige and tradition
- **Wordmark** — "GRACE" in Gotham Bold, "LONG LIVE LEARN" in spaced uppercase beneath

**Usage notes:**
- Preferred on dark backgrounds (`#10222b`)
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "G" on all sides
- Do not recolour, distort, or crop the emblem

## Design Principles

- **Authority** — The dark azure conveys depth, trust, and institutional gravitas.
- **Excellence** — Gold tones signal achievement, prestige, and academic tradition.
- **Growth** — The olive green subtly echoes nature, learning, and development.
- **Warmth** — The brown-orange grounding tone adds approachability to formality.
